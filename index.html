My Lovely Evenings

When I am stressed out, the evening sky with a cup of coffee acts as stress buster to my life. April 7, 2022

It was such a wonderful day which I spent in Vizag, I visited Cricket Stadium and Kailashgiri, and after that we had our lunch at CMR centre which is known for its delicious food items. For the matinee show we visited the jagadambha centre which has a history of super hit all time fav movies, the fan following of the movie is outrageous and I am still feeling like I can here all the voices. And after a rough afternoon, I finally came back to my room enjoying the city view with a hot brooming cup of coffee, that is when I catched the beautiful sky with my Camera.

MY CORINGA TRIP

A fun ride with my classmates April 2, 2020

Me and my class decided to go for a trip and finally fixed to visit Coringa Wild life sanctuary, which is a rushy place. The coringa park have a Glass bridge, Bird Eye view tower from which we can see the entire wild life sanctuary, and if we visited on the mornings we can also go for a boat ride. The boat ride is the most enjoyable part where I felt very happy with all my friends. We took a lot of selfies and pictures together. And coming to the wildLife part the sanctuary has a wide variety of animals.
